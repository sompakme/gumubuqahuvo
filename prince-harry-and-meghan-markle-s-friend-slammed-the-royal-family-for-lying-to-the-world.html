The news of Kate Middleton's cancer diagnosis has shaken the world, but mostly, it's been a low blow for everyone who knows and loves the Princess personally. While it seems that most of the Royal Family, or at least the senior members, have been heavily involved in supporting the Princess through this painful process, the same cannot be said for Prince Harry and Meghan Markle.

As the relationship between the Duke and Duchess and the Prince and Princess of Wales is reportedly broken, a friend of Harry and Meghan decided to weigh in on the situation, and his input was not well received. He harshly criticized the way the Royal Family handled the crisis and said some mean words about the announcement.

Prince Harry And Meghan Markle's Friend Says The Royal Family 'Lied'

Christopher Bouzy is a tech entrepreneur who rose to fame after his appearance in the Duke and Duchess of Sussex's documentary, Harry & Meghan. After Kate Middleton's cancer diagnosis was revealed to the world, he caused controversy by slamming the Royal Family for the way they'd handled the announcement.

"The palace lied, and the British press happily helped them lie," the CEO said. "The countless 'conspiracy theory' headlines, while knowing a lot of what was being said was true."

He then took it a step further, going after the Princess' announcement, saying "This is really some North Korea/Trumpian type of propaganda."

As he was quickly faced with backlash, he added "I am sorry to hear Kate has cancer, and I hope she has a full recovery." But he didn't leave it there. Instead, he doubled down on how deceitful he thought the Royal Family had been, saying "It is also clear that all three earlier photos of her were fake, and the palace tried to cover it up."

Not long before these statements, he fueled conspiracy theories about the Princess of Wales's first public sighting after months in hiding. He wrote: "I've compiled a few older videos of William and Kate walking together, as well as the recent video from Windsor Farm. Take a look and decide for yourself if it’s Kate or not. In my opinion, we still haven’t seen Kate in public."

"We were told Kate was recovering and would resume her royal duties shortly," he continued. "Yet, in the recent video, she is holding a bag and moving briskly. If she’s capable of such activity, why isn’t she back to fulfilling her royal duties yet?"

The Relationship Between Prince Harry And The Royal Family Is Still Very Strained

They say tragedy brings families together, but that doesn't seem to be the case for the Royal Family. These controversial statements from Meghan Markle and Prince Harry's friend only serve to show people just how strained the relationship between the Duke and Duchess and the rest of the family truly is.

While the Royal Family has been working as a unit to handle Kate Middleton's diagnosis, Prince Harry and Meghan Markle have reportedly been kept out of the loop and learned about the Princess' health condition at the same time as the rest of the world.
