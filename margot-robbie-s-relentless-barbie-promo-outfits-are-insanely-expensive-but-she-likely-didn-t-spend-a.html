Margot Robbie had Barbie promo outfits that are not only insanely expensive but also symbolic of her status as a global star. The Australian actress, widely recognized for her roles in movies like The Wolf of Wall Street and Suicide Squad, recently took on the iconic role of Barbie in a new film that celebrates the beloved doll's influence and legacy. With her acting prowess, Robbie breathed life into a character that has inspired generations of children.

The movie saw her traveling across several cities worldwide, leaving an impact with her performance and dazzling promotional outfits. The buzz surrounding Robbie's attire during the Barbie movie's promotion isn't merely about fashion. Robbie's appearance in various cities became a talking point in itself. Interestingly, it is believed that the actress did not spend any of her own money on these outfits.

8 What Is Margot Robbie's Net Worth?

As reported by Celebrity Net Worth, Margot Robbie has a net worth of $40 million. She made an impressive $24 million between September 2018 and September 2019, positioning her among the top-earning actresses globally during that time. Her wealth is not just from acting, Robbie has also been involved in various brand endorsements.

A major milestone came in 2018 when she partnered with the prestigious fashion brand Chanel. She worked closely with them and eventually became the face of their special fragrance, Chanel Gabrielle Essence.

7 Margot Robbie Was Styled By Andrew Mukamal For The Barbie Tour

According to Harper Bazaar, Andrew Mukamal, a renowned stylist, was the creative mind behind Margot Robbie's appearance for the Barbie promotional tours. With a unique approach, he merged the iconic Barbie look with Robbie's style by crafting original designs, choosing rare vintage items, and even recreating some of Barbie's most famous looks.

The collaboration between Mukamal and Robbie resulted in an unforgettable fashion statement that resonated with the film's theme, beautifully reflecting the essence of Barbie through clothing.

6 Margot Robbie Wore A Vivienne Westwood Made Outfit In The London Barbie Premier

According to Metro, at the Barbie film's London premiere, Margot Robbie chose to wear an eye-catching gown designed by Vivienne Westwood. This particular dress was a Couture Moonlight gown in a soft blush pink color, echoing the famous 1960s Enchanted Evening Barbie. The dress made a bold statement with its tightly-fitted corset and elegant trailing side. Adding to the look was white fluffy tulle that adorned the shoulder area, creating a graceful touch.

Robbie's choice of accessories completed the outfit: long white gloves reached up to her elbows, a flower-shaped brooch was pinned at her waist, and her feet sparkled in shoes by Christian Louboutin.

5 Schiaparelli Dressed Margot Robbie For The LA Barbie Premiere

For the Los Angeles premiere, Margot Robbie turned heads wearing a special dress by Schiaparelli Haute Couture, striking a pose on the pink carpet. 4 Emilio Pucci Dressed Margot Robbie For The Barbie Mexico Tour

3 Margot Robbie Wore Moschino For A Barbie Press Conference In Seoul
