Margot Robbie is an Australian actress with an estimated net worth of $8 million.

Originating from Dalby, Queensland,  Margot Elise Robbie was born on the 2nd of July, 1990. At age 17, she went to Queensland to pursue a career in acting. In 2007, she finished her degree from Somerset College.

As a professional actress, among her first appearances were on the feature films "Vigilante" and "I.C.U.". In June 2008, she started appearing on the soap opera "Neighbours" which triggered the rise of her name to prominence. From a mere guest role, she went on to get a regular role on the soap, appearing from 2008 through 2011.

In 2013, she portrayed the character of Naomi Lapaglia, a role which earned her several nominations from renowned award giving bodies. She is currently working on a film titled "Suite française", which is actually an adaptation of the novel authored by Irène Némirovsky. In 2016, she is expected to appear on the movie "Tarzan" where she would play the role of Jane Porter.
