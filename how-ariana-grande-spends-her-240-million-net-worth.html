How Ariana Grande Spends Her $240 Million Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Highlights

  • Ariana Grande's net worth is $240 million, thanks to her success in the music industry.
  • Grande earns millions from album sales and performances, including a reported $8 million for her Coachella performance.
  • Despite her wealth, Grande still shops for groceries at Whole Foods and donates millions to charities, including mental health organizations.

Ariana Grande's net worth currently sits at a whopping $240 million, and it's all thanks to her success in the music industry. The star first came to prominence on Broadway before landing her role as Cat Valentine on Victorious.

Following her days on Nickelodeon, Ariana Grande went on to become one of the biggest pop stars in the world. With her debut album, Yours Truly released in 2013 cementing the star as one worth watching, her other albums have only amassed millions more!

In addition to her singing, Grande was paid $8 million for her performance at Coachella in 2019, which is quite a pretty paycheck. That year alone, the star reportedly collected $50 million in total, earning herself $1 million for every show of her Sweetener tour.

So, with all these riches, what does Grande spend her millions on? Diamonds and lashes, of course!

If Ariana Grande Wants It, She Gets It (But She Remains Humble)

Ariana Grande does her own grocery shopping

While Grande's net worth is sky-high, the singer still keeps things somewhat normal, at least when it comes to her food shopping. And when Grande does her own grocery shopping, she opts for a pricey spot like Whole Foods!

Grande only "organic and vegan produce", hence her love for the grocer. In November 2013, the singer tweeted, "I love @wholefoods so much there actually aren't words. Just fruits and veggies and sesame tofu and friendly, peaceful people everywhere."

Users on Reddit have even reported running into the musician doing her own grocery shopping and acting comepletely normal.

Grande is plant-based and made that lifestyle change in 2018. With a diet as clean as hers, it's no surprise that it comes at quite a price! The change in lifestyle comes as a result of Grande claiming to love animals more than people. That, however, hasn't stopped Grande from wearing designer goods, some of which are made out of leather.

Pricey Real Estate Is Right Up Ariana Grande's Alley

Ariana Grande owns property in California and New York

Ariana Grande Donates $5 Million For Mental Health

Ariana Grande gives back to various charities

Grande truly is the gift that just keeps on giving! The Thank U, Next singer donated $1 million to Better Help for fans and the public to seek free therapy. Then in 2021, Grande upped the ante and donated a whopping $5 million in free therapy in honor of World Mental Health Day.

Since then, Grande has donated $1.5 million to organizations committed to protecting and defending trans youth in 2021 and in the same year, she also donated 1,000 presents to a Manchester, England hospital at Christmastime.

There certainly are other organizations that Grande donates to that do not make the news. And with her generous spirit, it is hard to fault Grande for spending her money however she wants. After all, she has worked hard so she might as well play hard as well.
