Togolese footballer, Emmanuel Adebayor has a net worth of $25 million. Currently on loan at Tottenham Hotspur, Adebayor plays for Manchester City as a striker and also plays for the Togo national team. His wages are so high at £170,000 a week — that Tottenham and Manchester City are sharing them.  He spent the 2011-2012 season on loan to Spurs but still has two more years to run on his City contract. Adebayor survived the armed attack on the Togo team bus at the 2010 Africa Cup of Nations in Angola that left three people dead. Adebayor has played for Metz, Monaco and Arsenal and was voted African Footballer of the Year for 2008.
