Pregnant Mila Kunis Wears Loose Tank, Skinny Jeans to Dodgers Game

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Taking baby out to the ball game! Pregnant Mila Kunis and her fiance, Ashton Kutcher, enjoyed a date night on Thursday, July 31, at Dodger Stadium in Los Angeles.

The Jupiter Ascending actress, 30, wore torn skinny jeans along with a loose tank—sans bra—that showed off her growing baby bump. Kutcher, 36, opted for an equally casual ensemble, sporting a Dodgers baseball cap, blue hoodie and matching Adidas sneakers.

After the game, the Two and a Half Men actor helped his wife-to-be into their black sports car.
