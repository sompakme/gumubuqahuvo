Was Ashley Tisdale Dumped By Her Ex-Boyfriend Because Of Taylor Swift?

Ashley Tisdale has, in recent years, made more headlines for her medical condition and her romantic life than she has with her career. Although Tisdale has been married to Christopher French since 2014, the star had been in a romantic relationship with musician, songwriter, and record producer Martin Johnson. Reports of Tisdale dating the former Boys Like Girls front-man began in early January 2012. As published by The Huffington Post, Tisdale and Johnson were spotted by the press and photographers leaving LAX in Los Angeles. According to the publication, the couple was returning to LA after spending a weekend together in New York City, wherein they celebrated New Year's Eve.

According to US Weekly, Ashley Tisdale and Martin Johnson first met after the former starred in the "Love Drunk" music video for Johnson's punk rock band. The publication also reported that a source close to the pair mentioned that the two began texting shortly after the music video wrapped production.

The relationship turned out to be one of the most controversial moments in Tisdale's short-lived career in entertainment.
