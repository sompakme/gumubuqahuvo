Bubba Sparxxx is an American rapper with an estimated net worth of $800 thousand. Born Warren Anderson Mathis, he later took on the stage name Bubba Sparxxx as it seemed more appropriate for his career. He accumulated part of his net worth by releasing hit singles like Back in the Mud, Ms. New Booty, and Ugly.

He was born in LaGrange in Georgia but later moved to Athens where Bobby Stamps introduced Mathis to rap. Stamps eventually became business partners with Sparxxx and also started managing him. Initially, Stamps got him work with several artists in Athens and Atlanta so Bubba Sparxxx could complete his first album. Jimmy Iovine from Interscope Records took notice of Sparxxx’s talent and signed him on so he could work with the likes of Organized Noize and Timbaland. His songs are frequently played at basketball and football games in the United States.

Later on, Sparxxx signed a contract with Virgin Records before establishing his own label called New South Entertainment. E1 handles the distribution for his label currently.
