Jennifer Love Hewitt Welcomes Baby Boy With Husband Brian Hallisay

Publish date: 2024-07-09

More to love! Jennifer Love Hewitt and Brian Hallisay have welcomed their second child together, Us Weekly can confirm. The couple's baby boy, Atticus, arrived on Wednesday, June 24.

Us broke the news in January that Hewitt and the hunky Revenge actor, both 36, were expecting baby No. 2. The couple's new addition now joins big sister Autumn James, 19 months.

The Criminal Minds actress opened up about her "magical" second pregnancy in the May issue of American Baby magazine. "I'm grateful. It's not lost on me what a gift it is to be able to get pregnant," she said at the time.
