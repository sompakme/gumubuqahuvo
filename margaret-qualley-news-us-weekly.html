Margaret Qualley News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Name:Margaret Qualley

Hometown:Kalispell, Montana

Birthday:October 23, 1994

About

Born October 23, 1994, to actress Andie MacDowell and former model Paul Qualley, Margaret Qualley made her acting debut with a minor role in the 2013 drama Palo Alto. She gained recognition for playing troubled teenager Jill Garvey in the HBO Series The Leftovers from 2014-2017.

In 2019, Qualley was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award for her portrayal of Ann Reinking in the FX miniseries Fosse/Verdon. That same year, she appeared in the Quentin Tarantino film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. The model turned actress was nominated for a Golden Globe Award in 2021 for her starring role in the Netflix series Maid based on Stephanie Land’s memoir.

Although Qualley has seldom addressed her romantic relationships, the trained ballerina has been linked to Nat Wolff, Shia LaBeouf and Pete Davidson.  She has been in a relationship with musician and producer Jack Antonoff since 2021. In May 2022, the Montana native seemingly confirmed the pair’s engagement via Instagram with photos of her wearing a diamond ring with her arms around the Bleachers frontman.
