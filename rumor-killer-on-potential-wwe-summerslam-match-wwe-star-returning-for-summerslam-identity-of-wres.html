Rumor Killer on Potential WWE SummerSlam Match

Following this year’s men’s Money in the Bank ladder match, there has been speculation that AJ Styles vs Shinsuke Nakamura might take place at WWE SummerSlam this year. However, according to Dave Meltzer of Wrestling Observer Radio, Styles vs Nakamura is not the direction in which WWE is going for SummerSlam, and it’s more likely that AJ Styles vs John Cena is in the works for the PPV.
