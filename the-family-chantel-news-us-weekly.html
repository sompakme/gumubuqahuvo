The Family Chantel News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Network:TLC

TLC’s The Family Chantel follows former 90 Day Fiancé couple Chantel Everett and Pedro Jimeno, who met in the Dominican Republic in 2016 and wed after their 90 Day engagement. Chantel and Pedro split in 2022 after five years of marriage.

Chantel and Pedro work through their drama on camera with Chantel’s family, Pedro’s mother, Lidia, and his sister, Nicole making regular appearances.

The Family Chantel premiered in 2019 and aired for five seasons until 2023.
