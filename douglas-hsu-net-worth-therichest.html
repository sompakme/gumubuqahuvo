Taiwanese businessman Douglas Hsu has an estimated net worth of $1.2 billion in 2015 according to Forbes. Currently, Douglas Hsu sits as the Chairman of Far Eastern Group, a conglomerate of seven publicly traded companies.

In the recent year that passed, Douglas Hsu's net worth has increased by $150 million due to the higher stock prices for Asia Cement and FarEasTone. He also owns other businesses which include Far Eastern International Bank, Oriental Union Chemical, and Far East Department Store.

Douglas Hsu is an alumnus of Wenzhou Normal College and Notre Dame University. Forbes reports that he is married with three children.
