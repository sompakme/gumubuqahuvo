Action Bronson Goes Down Under In The Season Premiere Of F*ck Thats Delicious

Publish date: 2024-07-18

The progression of Action Bronson‘s ‘Fuck, That’s Delicious’ series is impressive to say the least. After traveling across the world while smoking the most fire weed and tasting the best dishes he can get his hands on, Bam Bam’s food-based web series has developed into VICELAND‘s newest television show, which made its grand debut last night (Sept. 1). In the network premiere, the Blue Chips rapper brings Meyhem Lauren to the other side of the globe to explore Australia’s prime cuisine.

After turning up with his Aussie fans during a performance in Sydney, Bronson and Lauren toured the capital on a yacht while enjoying the tastiest gelato in the country. Throughout their latest culinary conquest, they also chomped down on the best fried chicken in the Southern hemisphere, and finally smoke the highest grade kush the Queens native has ever had down under. VICELAND’s “Fuck, That’s Delicious” airs new episodes every Thursday at 10 PM EST.
