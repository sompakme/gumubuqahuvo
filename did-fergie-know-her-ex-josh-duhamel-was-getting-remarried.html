Did Fergie Know Her Ex Josh Duhamel Was Getting Remarried?

Publish date: 2024-07-08

A well-known rapper and singer, Fergie also writes songs, performs, and produces her own fragrances. She spent a considerable amount of time with The Black Eyed Peas before venturing out on her own and finding success as a solo artist. In addition to being well-known for her music, she was in a well-known and well-publicized relationship with actor Josh Duhamel.

After they had been dating since 2004, they got engaged in 2007, married in 2009, and had their son in 2013. Even though this famous couple is now divorced, they both are co-parenting when they aren't in the spotlight. The couple has maintained a good relationship since their breakup. Also, Fergie has since continued to support her ex-husband in his endeavors. But did she ever know Josh was getting married? What was her reaction after finding out about his wedding?

What Was Fergie’s Reaction To Her Ex-Husband’s Engagement?

On his Instagram earlier this year, Josh Duhamel announced his engagement to Audra Mari. In the Instagram caption, Josh wrote in January this year: “It’s on!! She found a message in a bottle that washed ashore and said YES!!”

The post featured a photo of the pair holding up a piece of paper, seemingly at a beach. The message on the paper can be seen to read: "Audra Diane Mari. Will you marry me?"

Audra also commented on Josh's photo, saying, "I love you!!" She also posted their engagement photo on her Instagram, captioning it by saying, "my heart is so happy.. I love you @joshduhamel."

The actor was officially tying the knot for the second time after his divorce from singer Fergie.

The couple has since received well-wishes from a lot of people in the entertainment industry, including one from Josh’s ex-wife, Fergie. The Grammy Award singer sent her congratulations to the couple on their engagement and even provided a few green heart emojis.

Report has it that Fergie was full support to her ex-husband marrying Audra. A close source of the singer revealed to the publication: “Fergie is happy about Josh and Audra. Fergie has met Audra several times and thinks she is an amazing woman.”

The source added, “Fergie and Josh have such an incredible co-parenting situation and they have much love for each other. They will always consider each other family and their son Axl knows this. He sees his father a lot and has gotten closer to his dad’s new wife.”

How Is Fergie And Ex-Husband Josh Duhamel’s Relationship Now?

Josh Duhamel and Fergie have distinct future goals, which is understandable given how they grew apart.

Despite Josh's desire to expand his family, Fergie has continued to put her job and her son first. After their separation, the ex-couple kept the good vibes between them and shared a strong co-parenting relationship.

Fergie once said about their cordial relationship, “We’re great friends, Josh and I; there’s so much love that we have for each other. We really like each other; we have fun with each other. We’re just not romantic couple anymore.”

The romance between the two was the only thing that changed for the couple. The singer-rapper stated they would co-parent and still consider their extended families to be a part of them.
