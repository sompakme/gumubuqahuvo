Taylor Swift Fans Rejoice As Rival Scooter Braun 'Separates' From Wife

Publish date: 2024-09-17

Music mogul Scooter Braun has reportedly separated from his wife Yael Cohen after seven years of marriage.

A source told Page Six that although the pair have separated they do not intend to get divorced. TMZ, meanwhile, is reporting that the couple are still living in the same house, despite more than a month of marital trouble.

Braun represents such top pop stars as Justin Bieber, Ariana Grande and Demi Lovato. The music manager and Yael, a mining heiress who hails from South Africa, share three children - Jagger, six, Levi, four, and Hart, two.

Scooter, whose real name is Scott, posted a black-and-white snapshot from his and Yael's wedding to mark their wedding anniversary this Tuesday.

"If just for the kids you have given me everything. But thanks to you I have grown, I have been pushed to be the best version of myself and to continue growing and learning," he gushed in his caption.

"That all happened because you came in to my life. 7 years. The adventure is just beginning. Thank you Yael. I love you . Happy anniversary."

On last week's episode of The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills Erika Jayne denied rumors that she had an affair with Scooter.

Singer Taylor Swift has been locked in a bitter feud with Braun after he bought her recording masters from Big Machine Records. He then sold them to an investment firm for a reported $300 million. The feud has intensified since Swift decided to re-record her catalog so the original recordings are worthless.

After news of Scooter and Yael's alleged marital problems emerged online, some die hard "Swifties" took to social media to celebrate.

"'The world moves on another day another drama, not for me all I think about is karma'"- Taylor Swift once sang," one fan wrote online.

"She was probably team Taylor swift," a second added.

"Give Taylor her music back ok," a third chimed in.
