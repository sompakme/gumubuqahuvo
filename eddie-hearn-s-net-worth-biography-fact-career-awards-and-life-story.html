Eddie Hearn net worth and salary: Eddie Hearn is an English boxing promoter who has a net worth of $50 million. Eddie Hearn was born in Essex, England in June 1979. He is a director of the Professional Darts Corporation and is also a group managing director of Matchroom Sport and Matchroom Boxing. The company Matchroom Sport was founded by Eddie’s father Barry Hearn. Eddie Hearn focuses on fulfilling the company’s television boxing deal with Sky Sports in the UK. Eddie Hearn revealed boxing’s first $1 billion deal in 2018 as Matchroom signed an agreement with DAZN to stream shows in America. Hearn previously worked with IFLTV and is popular on YouTube. Eddie Hearn has promoted many world boxing champions and highly ranked fighters including Amir Khan, Anthony Joshua, Oleksandr Usyk, Kell Brook, Khalid Yafai, Ryan Burnett, Dillian Whyte, Artur Beterbiev, Danny Jacobs, Luke Campbell, Demetrius Andrade, Tony Bellew, Maurice Hooker, Tevin Farmer, Katie Taylor, Devin Haney, Rocky Fielding, Callum Smith, and more.
