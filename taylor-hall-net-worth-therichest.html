Taylor Hall is a Canadian profexsional ice hockey player who has an estimated net worth of $12.3 million.

Taylor Hall started playing ice hockey with his hometown, Calgary. In 2007, he was chosen by the Windsor Spitfires during the 2007 Ontario Hockey League Priority Selection.

While with the Spitfires, he was selected to represent OHL in the ADT Canada-Russia Challenge. Hall was hailed as the recipient of Wayne Gretzky 99 Award in 2009.

In 2010 NHL Entry Draft, Hall was selected as the first overall pick by the Edmonton Oilers. During the 2011 NHl All-Star Game SuperSkills Competition, he got the second place in the fastest skater feat.
