Hotel Costa Real, La Serena, Chile

Publish date: 2024-09-19

Located in the heart of La Serena, Hotel Costa Real is a hotel with bar, swimming pool, outdoor pool and a 4.3 guest reviews rating.

Hotel Costa Real has one of the most exquisite menus in the city, the on site restaurant treats guests to really fine dining. Guests can also unwind at the bar and enjoy an exotic drink.

Hotel Costa Real has family friendly rooms that are well-spaced and are centrally air conditioned. High speed complimentary Wi-Fi access, flat screen TV, mini bar, tea & coffee making facilities.
