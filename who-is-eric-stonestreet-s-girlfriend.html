Who Is Eric Stonestreet's Girlfriend?

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Eric Stonestreet, who appeared on the hit television show "Modern Family" as the incomparable, Cameron Tucker, has found himself a modern romance! The actor began dating his now long-time girlfriend back in 2017 and the duo has since been doing better than ever before!

Stonestreet met girlfriend, Lindsay Schweitzer, at a charity event back in 2017 and the two reportedly hit it off instantly! Flash forward a few weeks and the duo was coupled up, and happily so! While they definitely prefer to keep their relationship as private as possible, we've managed to do a little digging and get the inside scoop on just who Lindsay Schweitzer is. Let's get into it!

Who Is Lindsay Schweitzer?

Lindsay Schweitzer is the long-time girlfriend to "Modern Family" actor, Eric Stonestreet. While we love ourselves some Cam and Mitch, we are loving this even more! Stonestreet and Schweitzer met back in 2017 at a celebrity fundraiser event called Big Slick in Kansas City. The weekend saw a number of familiar faces including Stonestreet's, Paul Rudd and Rob Riggle.

The two instantly hit it off and began dating a few weeks later! While Lindsay me be dating an actor from one of the most successful shows in ABC history, she has remained out of the spotlight. Schweitzer has attended only a few major red carpet events alongside Eric Stonestreet, but for the most part, she chooses to avoid the limelight as much as she can. This most likely stems from not being a part of the entertainment industry! Schweitzer is a pediatric nurse, and while we aren't sure which city she works out of, we can only assume it is Los Angeles, as this is where Stonestreet resides.

A Match Made In Heaven

Eric Stonestreet has not has very much luck when it comes to his personal life and dating. The actor was romantically linked to Skinny Girl owner, Bethenny Frankel prior to dating Schweitzer, however, their relationship fizzled and it appears as if the two were "better off as friends", despite Eric claiming she was a "wonderful kisser". Although that relationship may not have worked out, he has since struck gold!

When it comes to the connection Eric and Lindsay share, it goes without saying that she is certainly a great match for him and vice versa. Stonestreet has mentioned just how "lucky" he is to have met someone as great as Schweitzer and we definitely do not blame him! It didn't take much time for Eric and Lindsay to fall head over heels for one another, in fact, one friend said, "they are very happy to have met each other and are enjoying spending time together" a source tells PEOPLE Magazine. Despite their strong intent on keeping their private life very private, we are ecstatic to see the two very much happy and in love!
