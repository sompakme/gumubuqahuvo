    Compatibility of Pocket Tripod Pro

    The Pocket Tripod Pro is designed to be compatible with a wide range of devices, making it a versatile tool for various smartphone users. It comes with a set of brackets in different sizes specifically tailored to fit different smartphone models, ensuring a secure and stable fit. To determine the best brackets for their device, customers appreciate the inclusion of a Grey Fit Card. This card helps users match their smartphone with the correct bracket size, ensuring a perfect fit and optimal functionality.

The brackets may require some force during installation, which can make the process a bit challenging for some users. Additionally, there have been occasional concerns regarding potential scratches on the smartphone screen or screen protectors while attaching or detaching the brackets. While these issues are not widespread, it’s essential for users to handle the installation process with care to avoid any accidental damage.

    Overall, the compatibility of the Pocket Tripod Pro with a wide range of devices is a significant advantage.
