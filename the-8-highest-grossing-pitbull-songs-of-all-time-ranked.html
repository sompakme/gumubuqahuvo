The 8 Highest-Grossing Pitbull Songs Of All Time, Ranked

Pitbull, the Cuban-American rapper, singer, songwriter, businessman, and actor, has captivated audiences worldwide with his unique blend of reggaeton, Latin hip-hop, crunk, pop, and dance genres. With a career spanning over two decades, Pitbull has released an impressive discography, including 11 studio albums, four compilation albums, two remix albums, and 80 singles. His music has sold over 25 million albums and 100 million singles globally, solidifying his status as one of the most popular and successful musicians in the world today.

Pitbull's music has produced numerous chart-topping hits that have become anthems for parties, clubs, and sports events. This article delves into some of his highest-grossing songs, which have solidified his position as a household name and a musical icon.

Rain Over Me - 3 Million Copies

Released in 2011 as the third single from his album Planet Pit, Rain Over Me features Marc Anthony. This Latin dance-pop track, infused with salsa and house music elements, encourages listeners to embrace life's challenges and live in the moment. The song succeeded commercially, reaching number one in Belgium, Spain, Switzerland, and Latin America. It also secured 30th and 1st positions on the Billboard Hot 100 and the Hot Latin Songs chart, respectively.

I Know You Want Me (Calle Ocho) - 3.2 Million Copies

I Know You Want Me (Calle Ocho) was released in 2009 as the lead single from Pitbull's fourth studio album, Rebelution. It became a massive success, peaking at number two on the Billboard Hot 100 chart as per Fandom. With its infectious rhythm and memorable lyrics, I Know You Want Me has sold over 3.2 million copies, solidifying its place among Pitbull's highest-grossing songs.

Hotel Room Service - 3.2 Million Copies

Also, from the album Rebelution, Hotel Room Service was released in 2009 as the third single. This electro-pop track samples Push the Feeling On by Nightcrawlers and Rapper's Delight by The Sugarhill Gang. The song's lyrics revolve around having fun and partying in a hotel room with a lover. It achieved significant success, reaching number eight on the Billboard Hot 100 and topping charts in Australia, Canada, Germany, and New Zealand.

Feel This Moment - 5.4 Million Copies

Featuring Christina Aguilera, Feel This Moment was released in 2013 as the fourth single from Pitbull's album Global Warming. This dance-pop track resonates Take On Me by A-ha and celebrates life and seizing opportunities. The song received critical acclaim, reaching number eight on the Billboard Hot 100. It also topped the charts in Belgium, Denmark, Finland, and Norway.

International Love - 5.5 Million Copies

Unleashed upon the world in 2011, International Love emerged as the fifth single from Pitbull's acclaimed album, Planet Pit. This infectious dance-pop anthem, featuring the charismatic Chris Brown, represents Pitbull's worldly adventures and encounters with captivating women from diverse corners of the globe. The song's magnetic appeal transcended borders, ascending to the lofty position of number 13 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart while reigning supreme atop the musical landscapes of numerous nations.

On The Floor - 8 Million Copies

In a coveted collaboration, Pitbull joined forces with Jennifer Lopez for On The Floor, the electrifying lead single from her album Love? which graced the airwaves in 2011. This pulsating dance-pop masterpiece ingeniously weaves elements from the timeless melodies of Llorando se fue by Los Kjarkas and the infectious rhythm of Lambada by Kaoma. With an irresistible call to surrender oneself to the rhythm and embrace the joy of dance, the song soared to the impressive heights of number three on the Billboard Hot 100 while conquering the charts in more than 20 nations, including Australia, Brazil, Germany, Italy, and the United Kingdom.

Give Me Everything - 8.2 Million Copies

In a star-studded collaboration, Pitbull enlisted the talents of Ne-Yo, Afrojack, and Nayer for Give Me Everything, the exhilarating second single from his album Planet Pit, which burst onto the scene in 2011. This electrifying electro-pop gem is a powerful anthem, urging listeners to seize every moment and embrace life without reservation. The song's irresistible allure propelled it to the summit of the Billboard Hot 100 and the Hot Dance Club Songs chart while also reigning supreme in over 15 countries, as per The Statesman.

Timber - 9.6 Million Copies

In a collaboration that set the world ablaze, Pitbull joined forces with the vivacious Kesha for Timber, the lead single from his extended play Meltdown, which burst onto the scene in 2013. This captivating country-pop fusion ingeniously samples the timeless melodies of San Francisco Bay by Lee Oskar and the infectious beats of Twerk by DJ Snake and Lil Jon. With an irresistible invitation to revel in the wild abandon of a bar or club, the song ascended to the pinnacle of the Billboard Hot 100 and the Hot Dance Club Songs chart, according to Billboard. Its infectious charm also conquered the charts in over 20 countries, including Australia, Germany, Sweden, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom.

Pitbull's career has been marked by numerous hit songs and albums, showcasing his versatility and ability to create music that resonates with audiences worldwide. From Rain Over Me to Timber, each of his highest-grossing songs has left a mark on the music industry. With their infectious beats, catchy melodies, and commercial success, these songs have solidified Pitbull's position as one of the most successful artists of our time.
