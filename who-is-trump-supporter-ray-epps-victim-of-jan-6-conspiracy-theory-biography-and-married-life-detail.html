Ray Epps Wife Robyn Epps

Ray Epps and his wife run a business that hosts weddings and business functions at a five-acre property in Queen Creek, Arizona, close to Phoenix. Ray was raised on a cattle farm and worked as a contractor for the majority of his career.

His job took him to Las Vegas, where he met his wife Robyn. Robyn had hired Epps to repair leaky roofs at apartments owned by her employer.

In 2010, after returning to Queen Creek, the couple purchased a 5-acre farm and gave it the name Rocking R Farms.

Epps and his wife made the decision to convert their farm into a wedding venue after their farm began to suffer as a result of competitors selling grass-fed beef, 

After 2016, when “the bottom fell out of the American grass-fed beef market” due to foreign competition, they turned from raising beef to hosting events after first purchasing the farm to do so.

Epps is also listed as an officer for Patriot Holdings, LLC, a listed alternative commercial real estate business, along with Robyn.

On May 8, 2021, Epps and his wife Robyn were reportedly removed of their duties as officers, according to Open Corporates, The Open Database of The Corporate World.

Ray Epps Jan 6 Conspiracy Theory

Ray Epps and his wife Robyn Epps were selected to represent a conspiracy theory that, as it gained popularity, undermined their life.

Epps unwittingly became the spokesperson for a pro-Trump campaign that sought to spread the untrue narrative that the F.B.I. was responsible for the attack on the Capitol.

As a result of right-wing media characters and Republican lawmakers falsely labeling Epps as a secret government agent who assisted in planning the attack on the Capitol last year, Epps has endured a great deal of suffering over the past ten months.

He was compelled to liquidate both his Arizona house and business. He and his wife packed their stuff into shipping containers and relocated to a mobile home in the foothills of the Rockies out of fear for their safety and uncertainty about the future.

On January 6, Mr. Epps wasn’t just a bystander. He went to Washington to support Mr. Trump, was caught on camera inciting supporters to gather at the Capitol, and was present on the day of the attack.
