It was massive news when Kelly Clarkson won Season One of American Idol. She was a waitress who proved herself to be super talented, and her career has only shot up from there. From being a judge on The Voice to hosting The Kelly Clarkson Show, Kelly has shown that she has a talent for chatting with people and having fun, not just singing, and that everything Kelly touches turns to gold.

When Kelly got married in 2013, it looked like her personal life was just as successful as her personal. That is why when it was announced that Kelly was getting a divorce from Brandon Blackstock, fans were shocked. But like many celebrities who are having issues with their marriages, Kelly kept things quiet until things could no longer be kept under wraps, which is the one and only reason that the announcement was made.

Divorce is always a lot more complex when a couple has children. And while Kelly has talked about co-parenting, there are some other details about the divorce that fans should know about.

Keep reading to find out if Kelly Clarkson's divorce negatively impacted her net worth.

The divorce, which was announced in 2020, was finalized in March 2022. At that time, Kelly was awarded primary custody. Although Kelly has a hefty net worth, spending this amount monthly does put a dent into Kelly's fortune. Fortunately for Kelly, her spousal support payments will be coming to an end soon, so that will stop the massive monthly hemorrhaging she has grown accustomed to for the last couple of years.

Kelly also has an opportunity to make up that net worth, given that 'The Kelly Clarkson Show' which was recently moved from Los Angeles to New York City, has been renewed through 2025. But knowing Kelly, that is not all she has up her sleeves professionally, and she will likely be growing her net worth for years to come.

Kelly Clarkson Owes Spousal Support To Brandon Blackstock

Total Spousal Support Owed: $115,000 Per Month

Kelly Clarkson's salary from The Voice is very high, but she has to pay her ex-husband a lot of money each month.

While Kelly has a $45 million net worth, her divorce has definitely meant spending some serious cash. This is because Kelly owes Brandon $165,601 monthly in spousal and child support.

Kelly's child support payments will be $45,601 each month and her spousal support payments will be $115,000 per month. Kelly also had to pay a lump sum of $1.3 million to Brandon, so it's safe to say that definitely cuts into her net worth.

According to People, Brandon had asked for a lot more money. He wanted $135,000 for child support a spousal support payment of $301,000, which would have meant that Kelly would have paid him $436,000 each month.

The Cost Of Kelly Clarkson's Divorce

Cost Of Kelly Clarkson's Divorce: Approximately $2.5 Million

Kelly has also paid a lot of money in order to get divorced.

Kelly paid $1.18 million in legal fees. According to Radar, court documents show that she spent $100,000 along with $1,187,525 for fees and lawyers.

The judge said that Kelly had to give her ex-husband $250,000, so a forensic accountant could research the money that she has, and she also was told to give him $1 million to pay for his legal fees.

It's interesting to compare celebrity divorces to what other divorces cost. According to Business Insider, each spouse will typically pay $15,000. But when it comes to legal fees, the cost depends on how short the divorce is. If the divorce takes a long time, more money will be spent.

As for what celebrity divorces usually cost, some can get extremely pricey. According to Insider.com, the settlement in Guy Richie and Madonna's divorce was between $76 million and $92 million.
