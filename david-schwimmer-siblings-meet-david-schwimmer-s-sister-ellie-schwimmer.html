David Schwimmer siblings: Meet David Schwimmers sister Ellie Schwimmer

Publish date: 2024-07-31

The most well-known role for American actor David Shwimmer is that of “Ross Geller” in the hit sitcom “Friends.” David was born and reared in New York, but after earning his degree from the prestigious “Northwestern University” in the late 1980s, he relocated to Los Angeles in search of better employment opportunities.

He remained essentially jobless for the following few years. He first began receiving offers for important roles that reflected his talent in the 1990s. On the television show “The Wonder Years,” he made his complete acting debut. He then made his debut in the 1991 film “Flight of the Intruder.” He was chosen in 1994 to portray the illustrious character of “Ross Geller” in the sitcom “Friends.”

Ellie, his older sister, is named (born 1965).
