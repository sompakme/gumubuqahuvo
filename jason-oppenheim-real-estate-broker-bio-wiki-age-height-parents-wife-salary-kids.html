Who is Jason Oppenheim? Jason Oppenheim Biography and Wiki

Jason Oppenheim is a well-known American real estate broker and attorney. He is the President and founder of The Oppenheim Group, a real estate brokerage representing buyers and sellers of luxury properties in Los Angeles. Jason and his team of real estate agents at The Oppenheim Group are the subjects of the Netflix Original Series, Selling Sunset, which premiered on March 22, 2019.

The show features Oppenheim and his team as they sell luxe life to affluent and celebrity buyers in LA. In 2019, Jason was recognized by the Wall Street Journal/REAL Trends ”Best Real Estate Agents in America” as the No. 1 Agent in the Hollywood Hills/Sunset Strip, No. 3 Agent in Los Angeles, and the No. 22 Agent in the United States.

Jason Oppenheim Attorney, Lawyer

Jason served as an attorney at the international law firm, O’Melveny & Myers from 2003 to 2007. He acted as a representative for a wide variety of corporate clients.

Jason Oppenheim ESQ, Realtor, Real Estate, The Oppenheim Group

The Oppenheim Group was founded in 1889 as The Stem Realty Co. in Hollywood. Jason’s great-great-grandfather was one of the prominent real estate agents of early Los Angeles.

The Oppenheim Group is an expert real estate brokerage, serving aspiring buyers and sellers of property in Los Angeles. The group is comprised of a collection of talented realtors working under Jason. The firm seals hundreds of deals annually and has brokered many of the City’s largest residential sales.

Jason has been recognized as a top agent in Los Angeles by The Hollywood Reporter, among other magazines. His clientele consists of many high-profile figures such as Chloë Grace Mortez and Orlando Bloom. He has also sold properties to celebrities like Jessica Alba, Dakota Johnson, and Nicole Scherzinger, among others.

Jason Oppenheim Selling Sunset Show Netflix

Selling Sunset is an American unscripted documentary TV series on Netflix. The show premiered with eight episodes on 22 March 2019. Selling Sunset revolves around the high-end residential properties in Los Angeles, which are marketed by The Oppenheim Group.

The series was created and produced by Adam DiVello. The first season was released on March 22, 2019. The second season will be available on 27 June 2020.

The cast of the series is comprised of agents from The Oppenheim Group such as Brett Oppenheim, Mary Fitzgerald, Maya Vander, Romain Bonnet, and Jason Oppenheim.

Jason Oppenheim Wealth, Million Dollar Listings

The Oppenheim Group has managed to record a total of over $1 billion in sales and over $300 million in active listings. The firm seals over 100 deals a year.
