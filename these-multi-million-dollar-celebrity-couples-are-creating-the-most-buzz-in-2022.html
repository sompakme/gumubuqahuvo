2022 is in full swing and fans are looking to Hollywood's biggest stars to see what they're wearing, where they're going, and of course, who they're dating. Some of their relationships are romantic and enviable, some are blending their families together by introducing children from other relationships, and others are downright toxic, and bring a whole lot of drama to the table. Here are the hottest celebrity couples that are already creating a whole lot of buzz in 2022...

Machine Gun Kelly & Megan Fox

Machine Gun Kelly and Megan Fox have consistently put their wild, over-the-top relationship on full display for fans to see. They have been brazen with their public displays of affection, often stopping on the red carpet to lick one another's tongues, and ooze affection and adoration for one another. They're the life of every party, in spite of the fact that their electric chemistry generates some very cringey moments. Megan Fox has even gone so far as to wear Machine Gun Kelly's blood in a vile she wears around her neck.

In January, MGK proposed to Megan Fox and the two are now embarking on their wedding planning adventure. Machine Gun Kelly's net worth currently sits at $10 million, and Megan Fox has a $8 million empire. It's expected that their nuptials will be a lavish affair with a star-studded guest list.

Kim Kardashian & Pete Davidson

When Kim Kardashian took on the hosting role on Saturday Night Live in October 2021, she had no idea her personal life was going to be taking a huge turn. After working on a number of skits alongside Pete Davidson, it quickly became apparent that there were sparks flying between them. They tried low-key dating without making a formal announcement of their relationship status, but the media accelerated that process, and the secret is out.

They started off 2022 by taking a vacation together, and Pete Davidson and Kanye West have continued to battle over Kim's heart. The world is watching for all the wild twists and turns this relationship is sure to bring.

Kanye West & Julia Fox

Upon breaking up with Kim Kardashian, Kanye declared his intentions to get her back again, but in the same breath, he also started trying his luck in the dating scene. He was romantically connected to Irina Shayk at some point, and later moved on to date a young model named Vinetria.

Kanye's name is now splashed all over the headlines as a result of his newest relationship with model and actress Julia Fox. Fans have accused West of using Fox as a tool to make Kim Kardashian jealous, but the two seem to be going strong, and have since gone on to attend a number of special events together.

Channing Tatum & Zoe Kravitz

Channing Tatum and Zoe Kravitz are an unlikely couple that stunned fans with their relationship status. The two are actors in the same industry, but run in completely different circles, and at the time that their relationship announced, Zoe had just ended her previous marriage. This was a surprise union, in spite of the fact that the two were very close friends that eally bonded with one another on a deep level when they worked together on the set of Pussy Island. This was Kravtiz' directorial debut, and nobody thought it would come hand in hand with a hot new relationship. They clearly enjoy spending time with one another, and have been spotted doing 'cute couples things' such as dressing up in matching outfits.

Olivia Munn & John Mulaney

Actress Olivia Munn and stand-up comedian John Mulaney's relationship has been packed full of controversy since the first moment it was announced. Fans were completely stunned to hear that Olivia was intimately connected with Mulaney, especially considering he had just announced the end of his marriage to Anna Marie Tendler.  That was just the tip of the iceberg.

In September of 2021, Munn and Mulaney announced that they were expecting a child. They have gone on to welcome a baby boy named Malcolm Hiệp Mulaney into the world and fans are watching closely to see what happens next for this couple.

Anna Kendrick & Bill Hader

Anna Kendrick and Bill Hader were costars in the 2019 movie Noelle, and it was obvious to anyone looking that they were good friends. They got along well, and had lots in common, but they Anna was dating cinematographer Ben Richardson, and Hader was dating actress Rachel Bilson.

The world was stunned to discover Anna and Bill walking hand in hand, and it took a while to believe they were an item. Fans are still taking in this news and want to know all the juicy details of how this relationship developed and what lies in store for this new couple.

NeNe Leakes and Nyonisela Sioh

NeNe Leakes had freshly buried her husband Gregg Leakes, after being in a relationship with him for 25 years. Gregg passed away after a long battle with colon cancer, and NeNe had spent the last several months of his life tending to him as his primary caregiver. Headlines exploded when it was revealed that immediately after Gregg's death, NeNe was already intimately involved with Nyonisela Sioh.

The two are inseparable and routinely post selfies and vacation photos of themselves. There are questions surrounding Nyonisela possibly being a rebound relationship, and the press is looking closely at this relationship to see how it develops.

Macaulay Culkin and Brenda Song

Mccaulay Culkin has one of the most recognizable faces in the world of entertainment. The beloved Home Alone star is a pillar of nostalgia for fans across the globe, who are delighted to see that his long-term relationship with Brenda Song has moved to the next level. The two have been dating for four years and seemed to be in a comfortable state in their relationship, when Culkin announced just days ago that the has popped the question. They are already parents to a little boy named Dakota who was born in April of 2021, and are now about to tie the knot to formally become a family unit.

Jodie Sweetin and Mescal Wasilewski

Full House alum Jodie Sweetin stole the hearts of fans with her innocent, adorable face while starring on the hit show in the late 80s and into the 90s. She has now also stolen the heart of Mescal Wasilewski, too. This adorable couple announced their engagement on January 17th, 2022, as Sweetin took to Instagram to broadcast her big news. “I love you Mescal, for always. You’re my person. I can’t wait to see the life that lies ahead for us,” she wrote. Fans are excited to see what their wedding will be like, and are delighted to see Sweetin advance to the next stage of her life.

Simon Cowell and Lauren Silverman

Simon Cowell has been very guarded with his $600 million net worth, but he will soon have to kiss a good chunk of that fortune goodbye, as he gets ready to throw a wedding reception with Lauren Silverman. The couple has enjoyed a lavish lifestyle together for 13 years and already share a 7 year old son. They have definitely waited a long time to take this step towards formalizing their relationship, and fans are excited to see what the guest list will look like for this lavish celebration.

Simon proposed on Christmas Eve, in 2021, and has really kick-started 2022 with this huge news.
