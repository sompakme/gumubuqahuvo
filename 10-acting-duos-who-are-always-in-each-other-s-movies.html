10 Acting Duos Who Are Always In Each Other's Movies

Publish date: 2024-09-04

One vital thing when it comes to pairing actors together is chemistry. No matter how great two people are at what they do individually, if their styles don't fit together, they won't be able to make it work. So, whenever two people find they can have that artistic connection, it's normal that they want to keep working together.

RELATED: 10 Actors Adam Sandler Always Has In His Movies

All the actors featured in this article share a unique bond, whether it's personal or professional, that makes them the perfect acting partner for the other. From old friends who grew up together, to actors who only see each other on set but it's always magical, here are some amazing acting duos.

10 Ben Affleck & Matt Damon

One of the greatest acting duos in the entertainment industry, and one of the greatest bromances in history. Ben Affleck and Matt Damon have been acting together for years. They knew each other since they were kids, and created Good Will Hunting while they were in college. After that, they worked in many amazing projects together, such as Project Greenlight, The Runner, Chasing Amy, Dogma, and Jay and Silent Bob. To this day, they are very close friends and love to work together.

9 Helena Bonham Carter & Johnny Depp

Helena Bonham Carter and Johnny Depp are a package deal at this point. Their chemistry when they work together is amazing, and added to that, they are very good friends. Helena and her then-partner, the one and only Tim Burton, teamed up with Johnny in many amazing movies, such as Alice in Wonderland and Alice: Through the Looking Glass, Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, and many other. Johnny is also the godfather to Helena and Tim's children.

8 Emma Stone & Ryan Gosling

When thinking of Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling working, most people will probably remember the great musical, La La Land. While that was an incredible project that perfectly portrayed their brilliant acting dynamic, Emma and Ryan have been working together for years.

RELATED: Emma Stone's Best Movies Ranked According To IMDb

They starred together in Crazy, Stupid, Love and Gangster Squad, and have since become very close. Emma said that he's "a dear, wonderful friend. I can't even imagine what my life would be without Ryan."

7 Meg Ryan & Tom Hanks

Readers might remember this duo from movies like You've Got Mail, Sleepless in Seattle, Joe Versus the Volcano, and the 2015 movie that Meg Ryan directed, Ithaca. Meg and Tom Hanks have worked together for many years, and one of the reasons why they have such a great connection is because they have a similar approach to working on set.

"He's just so easy. He listens; he roots for other people. (He) doesn't like there to be drama," Meg said. "I feel the same way. We're just really there to have fun, this is supposed to be a creative experience and there's no reason to get heavy."

6 Adam Sandler & Rob Schneider

This is probably the most important duo in comedy movies. Rob Schneider met Adam Sandler when he went to see Adam's first ever comedy show, and it was evident from the moment they met that they shared the same sense of humor. The first time they worked together was in Saturday Night Live, and ever since they have done countless movies. Big Daddy, Click, Grown Ups, You Don't Mess with the Zohan, to name a few.

5 Jennifer Lawrence & Bradley Cooper

Despite the age difference between Bradley Cooper and the star from The Hunger Games, Jennifer Lawrence, these two actors have an undeniable bond that allows them to collaborate incredibly well. They worked together in Serena, SIlver Linings Playbook, Joy, American Hustle, and others, and their connection remains no matter how much time they spend apart.

"We don’t talk often, but when I showed up in Boston for Joy, then all of a sudden it was like we never stopped," said Bradley. "We just started where we left off, and that’s rare. It’s just easy to look at her and feel like I’m telling the truth."

4 Chris Evans & Scarlett Johansson

Chris Evans and Scarlett Johansson share an intimacy and chemistry on set that can only be explained with the history they have. Before they became Captain America and Black Widow, the two of them had been working together for a few years.

The first time they shared a project was 2004, with the movie The Perfect Score. A few years laters, they did The Nanny Diaries, and there are rumor that the two of them will be working in the remake of Little Shop of Horrors.

3 Shailene Woodley & Ansel Elgort

In just a few years, these two young actors formed an amazing acting duo. Now, no one can see one of them without associating them with the other. The first time the world heard about this duo was when the film adaptation of the John Green novel, The Fault in Our Stars. There, they played two terminally ill cancer patients who fall in love and learn to spend their remaining time together. Shortly after, they played siblings in the three movies of the Divergent saga.

2 Emily Blunt & Meryl Streep

Emily Blunt and Meryl Streep's relationship has come a long way since they first worked together in The Devil Wears Prada. Meryl was already a movie star, but Emily has since grown into a star of her own, and the two of them worked in many projects together. In 2014, they reunited for the movie Into the Woods, and then, in 2018, they starred togethen in Mary Poppins Returns.

1 Al Pacino & Robert De Niro

Perhaps the most iconic acting duo in history, the Lennon/McCartney of the acting world. Al Pacino and Robert De Niro have been working together for many years, right until recently, when they starred in Martin Scorsese's movie The Irishman. The first time they co-starred in a movie was when they did The Godfather in 1972. Since then, they have done timeless projects such as Heat, Righteous Kill, and many more.
