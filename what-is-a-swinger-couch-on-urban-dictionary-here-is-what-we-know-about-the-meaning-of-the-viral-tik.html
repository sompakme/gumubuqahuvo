What Is A “Swinger Couch” In Urban Dictionary?

Mallory’s purchase from the Facebook Market Place is currently trending on TikTok, but what exactly is a swinger couch?

Swingers, according to Urban Dictionary, are those who participate in consensual sex with people or couples outside of their primary relationship.

Swapping partners or indulging in sexual practices in a group could be referred to as the word.

As a result, a “swinger couch” is a huge sofa where sexual behaviors are most likely to occur.

After purchasing a “round couch” from the Facebook Market Place, TikTok user @malloryonthemoon was confronted with an unpleasant reality.

Mallory took to the video-sharing network to tell her followers about her new couch, saying, she found that couch on the Facebook Market Place for $700. She had never seen a couch like that before.

According to her, she had moved into an apartment in Chicago and was looking for a funky couch.

The video has over 2 million likes and 10 million views, but not for the reasons you may imagine.
