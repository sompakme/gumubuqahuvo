The Cast Of 'The First Lady,' Ranked By Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-05

The First Lady is a new drama television series that first aired on April 17 of 2022. While there are several shows and movies that document historical leadership, whether as a mockumentary or simply historical fiction, this is among the first releases that tells the story through the eyes of the women in the White House.

This series brought on a talented cast, ranging from actors who have spent decades in Hollywood with several dozen projects to those who are relatively new and are using this title as their breakout role. Here is the cast of The First Lady ranked by their current net worth.

8 Lily Rabe Has A $2 Million Net Worth

Lily Rabe has been an actress since the early 2000s, starting her career in the film Never Again. Although she was hired to play “Lorena ‘Hick’ Hickock,” in The First Lady, Rabe’s claim to fame was through the hit television series American Horror Story. Between her work on big films likes Miss Stevens and No Reservations, Lily has been able to amass a net worth of $2 million.

7 Aaron Eckhart Has A Net Worth Of $12 Million

Aaron Eckhart was hired to play the part of “Gerald Ford” on The First Lady. He has been in Hollywood since 1992, when he acted in the movie Double Jeopardy. While he has been hired in over 50 projects, his most notable is his role in The Dark Knight in 2008. Eckhart currently has six works in progress, from pre-production to post-production, bringing his net worth to $12 million.

6 Dakota Fanning Has A $12 Million Net Worth

Dakota Fanning grew up on our screens, starting in Hollywood when she was only four years old. Before joining The First Lady, she had been involved with large franchises like Disney for Kim Possible: A Stitch in Time and Lilo & Stitch 2, the Twilight saga, and Oceans 8. She has most recently played “Susan Elizabeth Ford” in this series, and is currently working on two more projects, leading her net worth to $12 million.

5 Ellen Burstyn Is Worth $20 Million

Perhaps one of the most impressive resumes comes from Ellen Burstyn, who embodies “Sara Delano Roosevelt” in this show. She has been hired in over 150 projects since starting her acting career in 1958, including working on the film The Baby-Sitters Club, Interstellar, The Age of Adaline, and the 1973 movie The Exorcist. Burstyn’s net worth is now at $20 million, and she is currently working on four more films, one being a sequel/remake of The Exorcist.

4 Viola Davis Has A $25 Million Net Worth

Viola Davis was hired to play the infamous “Michelle Obama” on this series. She has been acting for over two decades, booking roles in Knight and Day, Eat Pray Love, Suicide Squad, and most recognizably she starred in How to Get Away with Murder. Davis has four projects in the works, and at least one of which set to release later this year. Her net worth now sits at $25 million.

3 Gillian Anderson Has A Net Worth Of $40 Million

Gillian Anderson is best known for her work on the beloved TV series The X-Files. She now plays the role of “Eleanor Roosevelt” on The First Lady, bringing in decades of experience. Her $40 million net worth is attributed to the popular titles she has been a part of, such as Netflix’sSex Education, The Crown, and Hannibal. Anderson also has two films and a video game in the works, and the movies will be released later this year.

2 Kiefer Sutherland Has A Net Worth Of $100 Million

The First Lady is the 101 project that Kiefer Sutherland has worked on since joining Hollywood, playing the role of “Franklin D. Roosevelt.” One of his most well-known roles comes from the television series 24 and its spin-off series 24: Live Another Day, as well as the franchise's numerous movies. Sutherland is now working on a short film, and his net worth is currently sitting at a comfortable $100 million.

1 Michelle Pfeiffer Is Worth $250 Million

The richest cast member of The First Lady is Michelle Pfeiffer, who plays “Betty Ford.” She has been in some major franchises throughout her career, including multiple Marvel films as “Janet Van Dyne/The Wasp,” in Disney’s Maleficent: Mistress of Evil, the musical film Hairspray, and DC’s Batman Returns as “Catwoman.” All of these have helped bring her net worth $250 million, and still climbing.
