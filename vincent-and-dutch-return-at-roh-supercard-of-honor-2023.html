Vincent And Dutch Return At ROH Supercard Of Honor 2023

Publish date: 2024-07-07

The Righteous is back in Ring of Honor.

During the Zero Hour pre-show about ROH Supercard of Honor, Stu Grayson of Dark Order defeated Slim J. Evil Uno accompanied Grayson to the ring, and the duo celebrated Grayson’s victory after the bout. They were interrupted by the return of Vincent and Bateman of The Righteous, who came to the stage and stared them down.

Bateman and Dutch are former ROH World Six-Man Tag Team Champions. They last appeared on ROH programming at Death Before Dishonor 2022, where they lost the titles to Dalton Castle and The Boys. They have not been featured on ROH TV, which returned in March.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
