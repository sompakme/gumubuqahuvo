Taylor Swift's Confusing Relationships With These Celebrities Rival Her Rumored Feud With Olivia Mun

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Highlights

  • Despite rumors, Taylor Swift did not feud with Olivia Munn, Justin Bieber, Demi Lovato, or Olivia Rodrigo.
  • Fans speculated on alleged conflicts with Swift based on interactions but no real evidence of fights.
  • Misunderstandings or jokes led to false feud rumors between Swift and other stars, but these were unfounded.

Given her place in the music industry, there are all kinds of rumors about Taylor Swift. Fans wondered about her exciting Travis Kelce romance before it was confirmed, wanted to know more about her most quotable song lyrics, and are forever curious about her ex-boyfriends who have a lot of money.

People also spread rumors that Taylor Swift is feuding with many celebrities. Of course, most of these stories aren't true, but that doesn't stop people from believing that she doesn't get along with most other stars. One of the most memorable rumors is that Swift and Olivia Munn don't like each other. In this article, we'll take a look at the stars Swift allegedly fought with, along with the reason why fans thought that this was the case.

6 Olivia Munn And Taylor Swift's Awards Show Joke Led To Feud Rumors

Olivia Munn Stars In The AppleTV+ Series Your Friends And Neighbors

Fans have been perplexed by Taylor Swift and Olivia Munn's relationship for a while now. Munn and Swift interacted at the 2013 People's Choice Awards and Munn acted like she was going to take Swift's award away. Since Kanye West famously interrupted Swift at the 2009 MTV Video Awards, Munn was alluding to that and later said it was meant to be funny.

People might have expected Swift and Munn to admit to a feud after this awards show moment. However, they never said that they were mad at each other or that they had any problems with their brief interaction. Of course, since people talk about everything that Swift does, it makes sense that people couldn't stop wondering about what really happened at the People's Choice Awards.

Justin Bieber and Taylor Swift may have a lot in common since they both got started in the music industry as young teenagers. According to CBC, Bieber's YouTube video got him noticed at 12. As it turns out, Bieber and Swift have a confusing relationship, just like Munn and Swift. No one is sure if they are friendly or not.

According to Nicki Swift, after the drama with Kanye West, Swift, and Kim Kardashian, Bieber shared a social media photo where he was chatting with Scooter Braun and West. He wrote, "Taylor Swift what up." After that, Bieber supported Braun when he bought Swift's masters.

4 Demi Lovato And Taylor Swift Reportedly Fought Since 2010

Demi Lovato Has Put Out Eight Albums

Demi Lovato is another star who has been the subject of Taylor Swift feud rumors that are as confusing as the rumors about Swift and Olivia Munn. According to Nicki Swift, fans believe that the two singers dislike each other because Lovato made several negative comments about Swift. When Swift gave Kesha $250,000 for legal fees during her legal fight against Dr. Luke, Lovato said that other people aren't in the same financial position. Lovato also said:

“Don't brand yourself a feminist if you don't do the work.”

While Lovato has many celebrity friends such as Sam Smith and Ariana Grande, according to Pop Sugar, the rumor that she was fighting with Swift continued for a long time. Swift doesn't appear to have talked about Lovato, just like she hasn't talked about Munn.

In September 2023, The Daily Mail reported that the two singers seemed fine with each other at the VMA Music Awards. Swift danced and had fun during Lovato's performance of "Cool For The Summer."
